Overview

Fog of War is a multiplayer real-time strategy game where players compete to conquer territory, capture enemy capitals, and become the last player standing. Inspired by classic strategy games, it features a unique fog-of-war mechanic that keeps you on your toes!

Game Features

Strategic Combat

Build armies, expand your territory, and attack enemy positions with tactical precision.

Fog of War

Only see what you've explored. Use lookout towers to extend your vision and scout enemy positions.

Bot API

Create your own AI bots with our comprehensive bot API and compete against other developers!

Multiplayer

Play with friends or strangers online. Host custom games with up to 8 players!

Strategic Objectives

Capture cities for army production, control lookout towers for vision, and defend your capital!

Cross-Platform

Play on desktop or mobile. Install as a PWA for the best experience!

Built With
