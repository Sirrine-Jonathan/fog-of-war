How to Play

Click Navigation
Click your territory to activate it. Click any visible tile to launch pathfinding movement. Hold Alt or Shift to activate without moving armies.
Keyboard Controls
Use arrow keys or WASD to move armies. Press spacebar to select your general. Navigate efficiently across the battlefield.
Map Navigation
Hold Shift and drag to pan the map. Use scroll wheel to zoom in and out. Explore the battlefield strategically.
Victory Conditions
Capture enemy generals to eliminate opponents. Manage your armies wisely - you need at least 1 army to hold territory.
