🚀 Quick Start

  1. Click on a game room below to join
  2. Enter your username and click "Join as Player"
  3. Wait for other players or add a bot
  4. The host can start the game when ready
  5. Conquer territories and eliminate opponents!

✨ Game Features

Real-time Strategy: Command armies in real-time battles with fog of war mechanics
Multiplayer Support: Play with friends or against AI bots
Host Controls: Transfer host privileges and manage game settings
Spectator Mode: Watch games in progress or continue viewing after elimination
Bot API: Create custom AI opponents using our Socket.IO API

🎮 How to Play

  • Movement: Click on your territory, then click an adjacent tile to move armies
  • Keyboard: Use arrow keys to move from selected territory
  • Conquest: Attack enemy territories or expand to neutral lands
  • Strategy: Manage your armies wisely - you need at least 1 army to hold territory
  • Victory: Eliminate all opponents by capturing their generals
