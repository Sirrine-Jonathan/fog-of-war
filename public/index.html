ðŸŽ® How to Play

  • Click Navigation:
    • Click your territory to activate it
    • Click any visible tile to launch an intent (pathfinding movement)
    • Alt+click only activates tiles without launching intents
    • If no active tile, clicking owned territory activates it, otherwise no action
  • Keyboard: Use arrow keys to move from selected territory. Press spacebar to select your general. Failed attacks keep the active tile selected.
  • Map Controls: Hold Shift and drag to pan the map (cursor shows 'grab' when Shift is held). Use scroll wheel to zoom in/out.
  • Conquest: Attack enemy territories or expand to neutral lands
  • Strategy: Manage your armies wisely - you need at least 1 army to hold territory
  • Victory: Eliminate all opponents by capturing their generals
